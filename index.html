ABOUT US

Get to know us!

Introduction

The Malaysian Public Library, Research and Development Centre (formerly known as the Malayan Public Library Association) was formed in 1955. Since then, this historical library has made immense contributions to the mental and intellectual development of Malaysians, both in the urban and non-urban areas. It has had a chequered history of providing reading materials and information services for the upliftment of the young and old through times of trouble and social instability.

Presently it is located on the 2nd floor, Wisma HELP, Jalan Dungun 50490 Kuala Lumpur.

The library, a non-profit organization, is managed by a Council of Board Members. The financial support comes mainly from members’ subscription fees and donations from the public. In 2002, HELP University College was invited to join the Council and to provide financial support as well as manpower to manage the library.
Currently, it has a collection of over 60,000 books, mostly in Chinese. The subjects cover a wide range from classical and modern literature to current topics of interest. Most of the non-Chinese books were donated by the library members and the Chinese community.
The management would like to encourage the Chinese community, students and other interested members of the public to come and enjoy the library’s full range of facilities as an important complement to their formal and informal education.

Facilities and the Future

The management plans to upgrade the library to help promote a strong reading culture, enhance educational standards, upgrade general knowledge and strengthen relationships among the various communities in Malaysia. The plans include:

  • Opening more reading areas.
  • Increasing the variety and size of the library collection.
  • Providing a computerized catalogue service to facilitate quick access to books and information.
  • Organising seminars and talks on important topics.

MEMBERSHIP

Become a member to enjoy our wide selection of books and aesthetic atmosphere!

How to become a Member?

Step 1: Show up at the location with the following documents.
  •  Photocopy of IC(MYCARD) (double-sided) or photocopy of birth certificate (for those under the age of 12)
  •  Photocopy of latest water bill or telephone bill (for verification of correspondence address)
  •  2 copies of passport size photo
Step 2: Make sure you have the registration and subsription fees.
Registration Fees  
          Student RM 2.00
          Others RM 5.00
Adult RM 5.00/month
Student/Retiree/OKU RM 3.00/month
Step 3: Watch out for the Rules and Regulations
  • Outstation members/ non-Malaysians need to pay a deposit of RM60.00 (refundable with official receipt)
  • Loans are limited to 4 books each time
    • The loan period is 1 month; renewals are permitted.
    • For overdue books, a charge of 10 sen per day per book is levied.
  • For those below 12 years of age, the parent must be an active member.
